# Seller Module

## Intake additions

Capture:
- products or services previously sold
- average ticket size and sales cycle
- inbound versus outbound experience
- CRM, phone, email, social, and field-sales skills
- compensation history and close-rate evidence
- existing network and target industry

## Ownership pathways

1. Independent appointment setting or lead generation.
2. Commission-based independent sales representation.
3. Productized sales service for small businesses.
4. Niche agency with repeatable acquisition system.
5. Sales team with documented scripts, CRM stages, coaching, and quality control.

## Skills to acquire

- prospect research
- offer positioning
- outreach writing
- discovery calls
- objection handling
- follow-up systems
- proposal and contract management
- CRM discipline
- pipeline forecasting
- recruiting and coaching representatives

## CEO transition markers

- Operator: can consistently book or close business.
- Contractor: owns a client relationship and gets paid under a clear agreement.
- Owner: has recurring accounts, documented scripts, and predictable pipeline generation.
- CEO: manages offer strategy, team performance, client economics, and market expansion.
